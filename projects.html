Little White Dress

Little White Dress was an idea born out of a real-world need; a close friend who is a wedding planner with a dream to open their own wedding dress store. A visitor to the LWD website can easily browse the products on sale, make a booking to attend the store and try on dresses, and join a community of brides who share recommendations. Built using the Django framework as well as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python. For this project I received a Merit.
